### 2. Data We Collect

The Bot only collects data that is technically required for its functionality:

### 3. Purpose of Data Collection

The collected data is used exclusively to:

- Create and manage support tickets  
- Associate tickets with users and servers  
- Ensure proper operation of the ticket system  

No data is used for advertising, tracking, analytics, or profiling.

### 4. Data Storage & Retention
